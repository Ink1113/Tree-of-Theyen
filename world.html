About World

Welcome to the world of Fruchi's! There are a plethora of diverse Fruchis! In the center of the continent is the Tree of Theyen. It is said that the tree was established by the first Fruchi referred to as Seedlin or The Giver. Once a year Fruchis travel to Theyen for a grand celebration full of dancing, singing, arts, and crafts. Also at this event research teams worldwide come together and trade their findings to gain more information and create new technology to help their fellow bunch members. Not only are Fruchis diverse in fruit they also have many traits. Standard fluff Furchi's usually live in forested areas as the forest provides some coolness so they don't overheat. In the desert shorter furred and scaled tend to live there as they can blend into their surroundings but also cool off in the heat of the day. On the coasts and islands, there are aquatic Fruchis, they live on land but can go into the water for gathering plants and having fun in the water (their favorite thing is to find the pretties shells and decorate their community). And last but not least the tundra, in this area, Fruchis tend to be super fluffy as they need to keep warm, it is also said that they are great huggers as they are super soft!
